How to Learn English

If you’d like to learn English, Lingoda is a great place to start. When learning any language, there are two main methods: working with a teacher in a group or individual class, or self-study, using books, online resources, and apps. The more time you invest in learning English, the quicker you will learn the language. There are four main elements to language: listening, speaking, reading, and writing. To learn a language well, it’s essential to cover these four bases.

Working with a teacher provides structure for language learning and a step-by-step process. This is particularly beneficial for beginners, as your teacher will guide you based on your language level and learning style.

For self-study, there are plenty of resources online about how to learn English effectively. Many language apps can also help you practice vocabulary and reading skills.

Is English Hard to Learn?

Generally, English is considered one of the easier languages for learners, especially if you speak a Germanic language like Dutch or German. However, it can be tricky for speakers of languages that are very different from English. The language contains many irregular verbs, spellings, and words that do not always match their pronunciation. The best way to learn English is through regular practice.

While self-study is effective, nothing beats regular conversation with a teacher or a native speaker to enhance your language learning experience!

What English Learning Levels are There?

In general, language learners are placed on the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) scale, which breaks down proficiency into four levels: A, B, C and D.

    A: Beginner
    B: Elementary
    C: Intermediate
    D: Upper-Intermediate

For your first lesson, you will take a placement test so that we can recommend the most suitable level for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to learn English? ▼
The best way to learn English is to practice speaking and listening regularly. Engage with native speakers, take online classes, and use language-learning apps for reinforcement.
How long does it take to learn English? ▼
The time it takes to learn English varies based on individual commitment and prior knowledge. However, with regular practice, many learners reach a conversational level within 6 months to a year.
Can I learn English for free? ▼
Yes, there are many free resources available online, including language apps, YouTube channels, and free courses. However, structured lessons with a teacher can greatly enhance your learning experience.
